Nao Nao Bar & Restaurant

Authentic Taste. Modern Vibes.

A Premium Afro-Chic Dining Experience

Nao Nao is a vibrant destination in Liberia, offering a blend of traditional local flavors and a trendy, modern atmosphere. The project involved creating a digital presence that reflects this unique "Afro-Chic" identity.

Key Features

Glassmorphism UI

Utilizes modern blurred transparent elements for a premium, high-end feel.

Interactive Menu

Showcases Liberian classics with custom-designed hover effects and responsive layouts.

My Role

I spearheaded the UI/UX redesign, moving from a standard layout to an immersive, dark-themed "Afro-Chic" aesthetic. I focused on performance optimization, responsive design using Bootstrap 5, and creating a seamless "Saturday Special" highlight section to drive customer engagement.
