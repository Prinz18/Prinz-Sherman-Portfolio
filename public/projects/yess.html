YESS Liberia

Social Innovation & Community Advocacy

Empowering the Future of Liberia

Youth Establishing a Safe Society (YESS) is a non-profit initiative dedicated to fostering a drug-free, safe, and productive environment for young people in Liberia.

Key Features

Anti-Drug Advocacy

Comprehensive campaigns targeting substance abuse and providing peer support systems.

Community Outreach

Direct engagement through workshops, educational supplies, and sanitation initiatives.

My Role

As the lead developer, I designed and implemented a high-performance web presence to increase global visibility, streamline donation inquiries, and showcase community impact. I also handled SEO optimization and Google Search Console integration to ensure the mission reaches a wider audience.
